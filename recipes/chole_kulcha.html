CHOLE KULCHE

Chole kulche is a renowned dish of India that is foind in the streets of India. Originated from the Punjab region, this dish is loved all over the country and is now making it's way in the west too. A delicious combination of an all-purpose flour bread tossed in butter along with tangy and spicy chole, chole kulche is one such dish that we Indians just cannot resist.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white peas
  • 2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry mango powder
  • 50 gm butter
  • 3 tomato
  • 2 green chilli
  • salt as required
  • 10 sprigs coriander leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

STEPS:

  1. Take a bowl and add 1 chopped onion, 2 tomatoes and green chili in the bowl. After that add 1 tbsp of bhuna jeera powder and red chilli in it.
  2. Add 1 tbsp of dhania powder, salt to taste, ½ tbsp of black salt and black pepper in the bowl.
  3. Then add diced boiled potato and 1½ cup of boiled chole in it. After that squeeze half lemon on top of the Chole.
  4. Add 1 tbsp of chaat masala and ½ tbsp of Kitchen king masala and also add 2 tbsp of tamarind chutney on it. You can see tamarind chutney recipe here.
  5. Now, give it a good mix and also mash some of the Chole (Matar) to give a mushy texture in the Chole Chaat. These all masalas and chutney give a lip-smacking flavor in the Chole. This Chole has full of tangy sweet and sour flavors. Now it’s time to toast a Kulche.
